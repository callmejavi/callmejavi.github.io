Why choose me for your next event?

  • Because I have an extensive knowledge of all kind of boardgames, having tried more than a thousand different titles along the past decade.

  • Because I'm always curious and I like to try everything, no matter if it is a 5-minute match of Rhino Hero or an 8-hour session of Twilight Imperium.

  • Because "knowing a game" and "knowing how to teach a game" are not the same thing, and I know very well how to teach games effectively.

  • Because I'm the go-to person in my group of friends when anyone asks for recommendations based on budget, number of players, length, complexity and theme.

  • Because I'm the go-to person in my gaming groups when anyone asks for someone to teach them a game or introduce them to new titles.

  • Because I can teach games in several languages.

  • Because I have been running showcase events and playtesting sessions for different publishers in several venues.

  • Because I have been attending game conventions and expos since 2014, working for both convention organisers as well as for publishers.
