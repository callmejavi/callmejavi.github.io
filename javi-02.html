  Why choose me?

  • Because I have an extensive knowledge of all kind of boardgames,
    having tried more than 900 different titles along the past decade.
  • Because I'm always curious and I like to try everything,
    no matter if it is a 5-minute match of Rhino Hero
    or an 8-hour session of Twilight Imperium.
  • Because "knowing a game" and "knowing how to teach a game"
    are not the same thing, and I know very well
    how to teach games effectively.
  • Because I'm the go-to person in my group of friends
    when anyone asks for recommendations based on budget,
    number of players, length, complexity and theme.
  • Because I'm the go-to person in my gaming groups
    when anyone asks for someone to teach them a game
    or introduce them to new titles.
  • Because I can teach games in several languages.
